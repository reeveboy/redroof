About Us

The Family at Red-Roofs” by Enid Blyton is a thrilling family adventure where Molly, Peter, Michael, and Shirley have their own adventure with their shares of ups and downs and happy endings. The hotel Red-Roof, established in 1995, got its name from this very book. This family-friendly hotel offers all the comforts you would need for an enjoyable holiday with your family. Rooms are equipped with all modern amenities like wifi, television, air conditioning, telephone, and a private bathroom with 24 hours hot water supply. The location of the hotel is very convenient for travelers coming by bus or train. The KTC bus stand is just a 5-minute walk and the Madgaon railway station is a 15 mins drive away. Goa is a land of sun and sand and a holiday at Goa is not complete without a visit to the beach. Hotel Red Roof is located close to the COLVA beach, MAJORDA beach, and BENAULIM beach. Read More
